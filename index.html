Yang Zhang    

I'm a Postdoctoral Researcher at UConn DSC lab, advised by Prof. Jiong Tang. My research interests lie in Computational Intelligence, AI-driven Engineering, Digital Twin, Inverse Analysis and Optimization, Mechatronics Synthesis with research vision of developing intelligent computational frameworks to bridge physics-based modeling, optimization, and machine learning for advanced engineering applications.
